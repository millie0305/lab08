Trivia

The Animal Crossing series is a popular game across the globe, so Isabelle has different names in different languages.
  • Japanese: しずえ Shizue
  • Korean: 여울 Yeo'ul
  • Chinese: 西施惠 Xīshīhuì
  • French: Marie
  • Spanish: Canela
  • Italian: Fuffi
  • Russian: Isabelle
More trivia about Isabelle:
  • Isabelle is immune to Pitfall Seeds in Animal Crossing: New Leaf, like all other special characters
  • A lazy villager might write a letter to the player saying they had a dream where Isabelle was 40 feet tall and was destroying everything
  • In Animal Crossing: Happy Home Designer, Digby and Isabelle claim to have spent at least a portion of their puphood in a cardboard box
  • In Animal Crossing: Happy Home Designer, Isabelle mentions having chewed on Digby's shoes when they were younger.
  • In Animal Crossing: Happy Home Designer, Digby will reveal that Isabelle is surprisingly smart, being graduated in engineering in nineteen different universities.
  • In Animal Crossing: Happy Home Designer, if the player cancels midway through Isabelle's home request, she will mention that she once worked with a mayor who erased a whole town.
  • Other than Digby, her twin brother, Isabelle also shares her birthday with Rudy. Oddly enough, they all debuted in New Leaf together.
